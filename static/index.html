How it works

1

Your agent calls a paid endpoint without payment headers.

2

Server responds 402 Payment Required with the price (in USDC) and the payTo address.

3

Agent signs an EIP-3009 transferWithAuthorization and re-sends with X-PAYMENT header.

4

Facilitator settles the USDC transfer on Base. Server returns the data.

Source & freshness

All data comes from MET Norway's public APIs — Locationforecast, Oceanforecast, MetAlerts, Sunrise. Forecasts are cached for 30 min, marine for 1 hr, alerts for 5 min. The response includes an X-Cache: HIT|MISS header so you know whether you got fresh data.

Coverage: Norway, Svalbard, Jan Mayen, and surrounding Scandinavian waters (latitude 55–81°N, longitude –10 to 35°E).
